About Bright Day School

Welcome to Bright Day School, a beacon of learning situated in the serene environment of Dhambola-Simalwara. Established with a vision to provide unparalleled English Medium education, we cater to students from Play Group to 8th Standard, fostering a journey of discovery, growth, and achievement.

Our philosophy is rooted in nurturing not just intelligent minds, but also compassionate and responsible individuals. We believe that true education extends beyond textbooks, encompassing character building, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning.

Our dedicated team of experienced educators is committed to creating an engaging and supportive atmosphere where every child feels valued and inspired to reach their full potential. We pride ourselves on a curriculum that blends academic rigor with a rich array of extracurricular activities, ensuring a well-rounded development.

Admissions Open 2025-26

Bright Day School is thrilled to announce that admissions are now open for the academic year 2025-26! We invite aspiring students and their families to become a part of our thriving educational community. We offer quality English Medium education from Play Group to 8th Standard.

Simple Admission Process:

  1. **Inquiry & Prospectus:** Visit our campus to collect the admission kit.
  2. **Registration & Form Submission:** Complete and submit the admission form with required documents.
  3. **Interaction/Assessment:** A brief session to assess the child's readiness (as per class).
  4. **Admission Confirmation:** Upon successful completion and fee payment.

Key Documents Required: Birth Certificate, Aadhar Cards (child & parents), Previous Report/TC (if applicable), Passport-sized photos.

For more details or to schedule a campus visit, please contact us!
