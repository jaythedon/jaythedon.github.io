About Bright Day School

Welcome to Bright Day School, a beacon of learning situated in the serene environment of Dhambola-Simalwara. Established with a vision to provide unparalleled English Medium education, we cater to students from Play Group to 8th Standard, fostering a journey of discovery, growth, and achievement.

Our philosophy is rooted in nurturing not just intelligent minds, but also compassionate and responsible individuals. We believe that true education extends beyond textbooks, encompassing character building, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning. Our dedicated team of experienced educators is committed to creating an engaging and supportive atmosphere where every child feels valued and inspired to reach their full potential.

At Bright Day School, we pride ourselves on a curriculum that blends academic rigor with a rich array of extracurricular activities, ensuring a well-rounded development. Our consistent track record of 100% board results stands as a testament to our unwavering commitment to academic excellence and the collaborative efforts of our students, teachers, and parents.

Admissions Open 2025-26

Bright Day School is thrilled to announce that admissions are now open for the academic year 2025-26! We invite aspiring students and their families to become a part of our thriving educational community. We offer quality English Medium education from Play Group to 8th Standard.

Admission Process:

  • **Step 1: Inquiry & Prospectus:** Visit our school campus during office hours (Monday-Saturday, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM) to collect the admission kit and prospectus. Our administrative staff will be happy to answer your queries.
  • **Step 2: Registration & Form Submission:** Complete the admission form diligently and submit it along with the required documents within the specified timeframe.
  • **Step 3: Interaction/Assessment:** A brief interaction session or assessment will be conducted for the child, as per the class requirement, to understand their readiness.
  • **Step 4: Admission Confirmation:** Upon successful completion of the process and payment of fees, your child's admission will be confirmed.

Documents Required:

  • Original Birth Certificate of the child.
  • Photocopy of the Aadhar Card of the child and parents.
  • Previous School Report Card / Transfer Certificate (if applicable, for Class 1 and above).
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs of the child.
  • Any other relevant documents as specified by the school.

For more details or to schedule a campus visit, please contact us!
